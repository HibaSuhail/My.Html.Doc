About Director

Nawab Saquib Ibrahim

Nawab Saquib Ibrahim is a prominent mathematics educator and author based in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh. He is the Founder and Director of Image Classes, a coaching institute that specializes in preparing students for entrance examinations such as those conducted by Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) and Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI). He is also the author of the widely used "AMU Booster" series, which offers chapter-wise and year-wise solved papers for various AMU entrance exams . Mr. Ibrahim holds both B.Tech and M.Tech degrees from Aligarh Muslim University, having completed his M.Tech in Instrumentation and Control in 2015 . With over 11 years of experience in mathematics education, he has taught at several institutions, including Sarwar Classes and Skill Classes, before establishing Image Classes .
